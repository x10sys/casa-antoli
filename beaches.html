Beach Green Lagoon

Funtana

Zelena Laguna Beach is located just a few kilometers away from the center of Poreč, situated in a beautiful bay among the green landscape. The beach consists of paved, rocky and pebbly parts and a separated part for nudists. The beach is clean and allows you to rent lounge chairs and parasols, as well as showers. Thanks to its safe nature and the crystal-clear sea, the beach was deservedly awarded with the Blue Flag. The beach offers a variety of interesting features, therefore you can relax by playing tennis, table tennis, beach volleyball and miniature golf. Near the beach there are various bars and restaurants where you can enjoy the summer atmosphere.

Beach Fjord

Lim Channel, Vrsar

Lim Channel, also known as Lim Bay or Lim Fjord is the part of the 35 km long Limska draga hollow that stretches almost to the town of Pazin-Pisino in the very heart of Istria. The channel itself is over 10 km long and surrounded on both sides by steep hills that can reach up to 100 m in height.

The water in the bay is partly brackish, because of its underwater sources of sweet water and it is thus suitable for the growth of plant and animal life and it is also a well-known fish and oyster farm (oysters, mussels, giltheads, sea basses).

All these delicacies can be tasted in two restaurants on the very coast of the bay. The bay surroundings are very interesting, the plant covering is lush and diverse, reaching from evergreen to deciduous trees, where various wild animals live, such as boars, fox, badgers, some roe deers and birds like hawks, buzzards, owls.

Free climbing lovers will find a suitable cliff on the northern side, where during the summer time fans of this extreme sport are doing their daily climbing tour.

Beach Lanterna

Tar

Lanterna beach is part of the camping site of the same name and is situated not far from Novigrad on the west coast of Istria. It is one of the few sandy beaches in the region and is very popular with campers.

As the beach is in a large bay, the sea here is calm and a popular anchoring spot for boats. On the camping site there are numerous restaurants, shops and a pool.

You can relax on one of the many sofas at the edge of the pool and listen to the music chosen by the DJ. In the evenings the music gets a bit louder and the dancing and partying starts.
